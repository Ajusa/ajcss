Welcome to AjCSS

Cloud computing. Designed for Developers

AjCSS is a framework built on both Spectre and Tachyons. It aims to try and bring the best of both worlds. By incorporating the pre built parts of Spectre and the atomic parts of Tachyons, it enables rapid simple development.
